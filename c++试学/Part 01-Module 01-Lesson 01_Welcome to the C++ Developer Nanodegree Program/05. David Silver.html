05. David Silver

David Silver

David Silver teaches throughout the Udacity C++ Developer Nanodegree Program, demonstrating programming examples.

David leads the Curriculum Team at Udacity, which build courses and Nanodegree programs in web programming, mobile programming, cloud computing, data science, artificial intelligence, and robotics. Prior to Udacity, David was a Research Engineer at Ford Motor Company. Before Ford, David worked in engineering and product roles at Candidate Metrics, mSpot, and AOL. He has an MBA from Stanford University, and a BSE in computer science from Princeton University.
